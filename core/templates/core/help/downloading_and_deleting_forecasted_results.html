Downloading and deleting forecasted results.

Download

  1. Ensure you're logged in to your account. If not, please log in here.
  2. Before downloading, make sure you've used the tool, as there won't be any results to download otherwise.
  3. Go to the tool page for your selected industry.
  4. Scroll to the "Forecasted Results" section at the bottom of the page.
  5. Here, the forecasted results are presented as downloadable links. Click on a link to begin the download.
  6. After downloading, you'll need to extract the files. Supported file formats are CSV and JSON.

Delete All

  1. As with downloading, ensure you're logged in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the tool page for your chosen industry.
  3. Scroll down to the "Forecasted Results" section.
  4. There, you'll find the "Clear All" button. CAUTION: THIS BUTTON WILL DELETE ALL FORECASTED RESULTS. PROCEED WITH CARE.
  5. Clicking "Clear All" will prompt you with a confirmation message, "Are you sure?"
