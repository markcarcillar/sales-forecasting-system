How to Register and Log in to an Account?

Register

  1. Navigate to the Registration Page by clicking this link: Register.
  2. Enter a username and password. Ensure you choose a strong password..
  3. Click the "Register" button.

Login

  1. Navigate to the Login Page by clicking this link: Login.
  2. Input your registered username and password.
  3. Click the "Login" button.
